Important Instructions

This extension is currently in beta. The Freshworks pricing pages are being changed dynamically and I would have to update the functionality based on these ad-hoc changes. Please reach out to me on Slack/Email for any quick feedback.

When viewing the price of addons which dependent on an option you choose, please ensure that you choose the value you want to pick and then click the extension to switch through the prices.

Upcoming features - Check Discount Percentage by right clicking, Additional currency support.
Please Slack me your feedback and ideas.
